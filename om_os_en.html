History

Norsminde Fishing House has existed for over 60 years, with many different tenants. The fishing house is the pearl of the harbor and is a popular eatery among the locals. There has always been a focus on serving fresh fish and the famous fish cakes.

In 2018, Fiskehuset received a major renovation and an extension to the restaurant. There is now room for more people both inside and out.

Who are we?

Signe Graham is 38 years old and is one of the tenants of Norsminde Fishing House. She is an unskilled waiter, and helps every day at the harbor. Before taking over the Norsminde Fish House, she and Rhodi Graham owned a small café and a bakery in Holland.

Rhodi Graham is 40 years old and originally from Australia. He is a trained chef, and provides daily for the home-made products that Fiskehuset offers. Together with his wife, Signe Graham, he helped manage the cafe and bakery in Holland.

Our values and mission;

"We are in favor of high quality at reasonable prices, that is the most important thing. We smoke our fish, bake our bread and the remoulade is made from the bottom. We are passionate about finding good ingredients, and you can taste that in the finished product.


The fisherman's house must be a place to come and enjoy life at the harbor and relax with a delicious plate of food and a beer or glass of wine. There must be room for children and adults, one should be able to get a quick fish cake to take home or enjoy a three course menu. The food will be both classic and modern, Danish and international. There must be something for everyone. "

- Signe Graham
